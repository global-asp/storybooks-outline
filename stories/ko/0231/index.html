날씨 책 Weather book Le livre de la météo

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Sandra McDougall, Ingrid Schechter

Jong Yong Park

Scarlet Kim

Korean

Level 1

건조해.

It is dry.

Il fait sec.

태양은 뜨거워.

The sun is hot.

Le soleil est chaud.

바람이 불어.

It is windy.

Il fait du vent.

구름이 꼈네.

It is cloudy.

Il y a des nuages.

추워.

It is cold.

Il fait froid.

비가 와.

It is raining.

Il pleut.

천둥이 쳐.

There is thunder.

Il y a du tonnerre.

난 무지개를 봐.

I see a rainbow.

Je vois un arc-en-ciel.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Sandra McDougall, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Jong Yong Park
Read by: Scarlet Kim
Language: Korean
Level 1
