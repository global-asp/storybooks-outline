Книга Про Погоду Weather book Le livre de la météo

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Sandra McDougall, Ingrid Schechter

Eve Mekhlis

Russian

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Сухо.

It is dry.

Il fait sec.

Солнце жаркое.

The sun is hot.

Le soleil est chaud.

Дует.

It is windy.

Il fait du vent.

Облачно.

It is cloudy.

Il y a des nuages.

Холодно.

It is cold.

Il fait froid.

Идёт дождь.

It is raining.

Il pleut.

Есть гром.

There is thunder.

Il y a du tonnerre.

Я вижу радугу.

I see a rainbow.

Je vois un arc-en-ciel.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Sandra McDougall, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Eve Mekhlis
Language: Russian
Level 1
