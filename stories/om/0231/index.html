Kitaaba Hala qilensaa Weather book Le livre de la météo

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Sandra McDougall, Ingrid Schechter

Demoze Degefa

Oromo

Level 1

Qilensii gogadha.

It is dry.

Il fait sec.

Aduun bayee ho’a.

The sun is hot.

Le soleil est chaud.

Bubee qaba.

It is windy.

Il fait du vent.

Dumeesa.

It is cloudy.

Il y a des nuages.

Niqabbana’a.

It is cold.

Il fait froid.

Ni rooba.

It is raining.

Il pleut.

Bakkakaa busee.

There is thunder.

Il y a du tonnerre.

Ani sabbata waqaab argee.

I see a rainbow.

Je vois un arc-en-ciel.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Sandra McDougall, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Demoze Degefa
Language: Oromo
Level 1
